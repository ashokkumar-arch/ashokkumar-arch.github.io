Series III — Cloud-Native Virtualization

OpenShift Virtualization
VM Migration & Platform Decommission

Migrating all VM workloads from VMware Cloud Foundation to OpenShift Virtualization (KubeVirt) on bare-metal, then fully decommissioning NSX and VCF — 12 guides covering bare-metal provisioning, ODF storage, VM networking, cold and warm migration, GitOps lifecycle, ACS security, ACM fleet governance, AAP guest OS automation, and the complete NSX exit.

12 guides
Environment: novacrestbank.local · 10.110–140.x.x
Compliance: PCI-DSS v4.0 · NIST SP 800-53
Status: Complete
Natural continuation of Series II. This series picks up where Series II ends — the VCF + NSX environment is running, OpenShift is installed, and the platform is operationally mature. Series III migrates every VM workload off ESXi onto bare-metal KubeVirt nodes, then systematically decommissions NSX (DFW → NetworkPolicy, T0 BGP → OVN EgressGateway, Edge nodes, NSX Manager) and VCF (vCenter, SDDC Manager, ESXi repurposing) — eliminating approximately $450,000 per year in VMware licensing. Series I or II experience is helpful context but not required; each guide is self-contained with environment references.
